Daniel John Bedingfield (born 3 December 1979) is an English-New Zealand singer-songwriter and record producer. He is the brother of fellow singers Natasha Bedingfield and Nikola Rachelle. He was a judge on The X Factor NZ in 2013.

"If You're Not the One" is a song by English recording artist Daniel Bedingfield. Written by Bedingfield and produced by Mark Taylor, it was released on 22 November 2002 as the third single from his debut studio album Gotta Get Thru This (2002).

The single entered the top 20 on the majority of charts that it appeared on, including being a number-one hit in the UK Singles Chart, and reaching number 15 in the United States.
